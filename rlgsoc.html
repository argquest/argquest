Köpenick Quest

Welcome to The Köpenick Quest

This is a location-based game. The game consists of 7 puzzles. The puzzles are linked to locations in Köpenick. Each puzzle gives you directions to the next one. To get a puzzle you have to scan a QR-code placed somewhere in Köpenick. So you have to be there to play this game. You will find coordinates to the first QR-code on this page.

The quest requires you to travel 9-12 km. So it's preferable to cycle.

Ready to play the game?

Strange QR-codes appear all over the city of Köpenick. The mayor wants you to find out where they are leading.
